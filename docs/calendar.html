Calendar for 500: Spring 2024

Modified

2024-03-05

This Calendar serves as the final word on all deadlines, deliverables and class sessions.

Readings

During the course, we will read Paul Rosenbaum’s book Causal Inference (abbreviated PRR CI in what follows), which is available as an e-book or in paperback for under $15. Please obtain the book before our first class on 2024-01-18, and, if you can, read Chapters 1-3 as soon as possible.

Timing and Location

  • All classes are held in Wolstein Research Building, Room 1217.
  • Zoom recordings will be made available when possible.
  • Classes 01, and 12-15 will begin at 8:30 AM and continue until 11:00 AM.
  • Classes 02-11 will begin at 9:00 AM and continue until 11:00 AM.

Email

Contact us at 500-help at case dot edu if you have any questions, once the class begins.

Office Hours

  • Our teaching assistant, Leila Hojat, will hold office hours via Zoom on Mondays and Wednesdays from 5 to 6 PM, starting on 2024-01-22 and continuing through 2024-05-01.
    • TA office hours are cancelled on 2024-03-11, 2024-03-13, 2024-04-01, 2024-04-03, 2024-04-08 (due to the eclipse) and 2024-04-17.
    • Zoom details for Leila’s office hours are posted to our Shared Google Drive and as an announcement on Canvas.
  • Dr. Love will hold office hours after class on Thursdays (11 AM to Noon). Just let him know during class if you want to speak with him afterwards.
  • For more information about us, visit Contact Us.
